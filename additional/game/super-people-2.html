About this game

This game is a new form of a Battle Royale Shooter.
SUPER PEOPLE 2 is a Battle Royale Shooter where you play as one of the Super Soldiers with various skills and compete to become the last man or last trio standing.

Make your character stronger in various ways as the game progresses. Class-specific skills and ultimates can be learned and used as your character levels up.

Experience the thrill and excitement of turning the tide in an instant with faster means to acquire ultimates. The ever-so-different gameplay for each round adds another flavor to its unique experience one cannot get from playing other Battle Royale shooters.

Engagements can occur at any time, anywhere, so the process of planning a route to get the best equipment can be seen as a strategy and a fun element.
